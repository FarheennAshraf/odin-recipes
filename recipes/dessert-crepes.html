Dessert Crepes

Description

This crepe recipe is essential for a fancy breakfast or eye-catching dessert. This simple but delicious crepe recipe can be made in minutes from ingredients that everyone has on hand. Sprinkle warm crepes with sugar and lemon, or serve with whipped cream, ice cream, and fruit.

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs, lightly beaten
  • 1 ⅓ cups milk
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • ½ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Whisk together eggs, milk, flour, melted butter, sugar, and salt in a large bowl until smooth.
  2. Heat a medium skillet or crepe pan over medium heat. Grease the pan with a small amount of butter or oil applied with a brush or paper towel.
  3. Using a serving spoon or small ladle, spoon about 3 tablespoons crepe batter onto the hot pan, tilting the pan to evenly coat the bottom surface. When bubbles form on the top and the edges are dry, flip over and cook until lightly browned on the other side and edges are golden.
  4. Fill the crepe with your favorite fruit, cream, caramel or even ice cream or cheese to serve.
