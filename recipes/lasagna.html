Lasagna

Description

This is a special homemade meat lasagna recipe, made-from-scratch tomato sauce and a deliciously cheesy filling. While a traditional homemade lasagna filling would be made with ricotta, this special homemade lasagna filling has a blend of small-curd cottage cheese and Parmesan. It is better-Served with a leafy green salad and crusty garlic bread.

Ingredients

  • Meat: ½ pound ground pork, ½ pound lean ground beef
  • Onion: ½ cup minced onion
  • Tomatoes: 1 (28 ounce) can crushed tomatoes, 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce
  • Fresh herbs: 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley (divided), 1½ teaspoons dried basil
  • Spices and seasonings: 1 clove garlic (crushed), 1 ½ teaspoons dried basil, ½ teaspoon dried oregano, ¼ teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Sugar & Salt: 1 teaspoons salt, ⅛ teaspoon white sugar
  • Noodles: 1 (16 ounce) package lasagna noodles
  • Cheese: 1 pound small-curd cottage cheese, ¾ cup grated Parmesan cheese, 1 (16 ounce) package shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Eggs: 3 large eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat: Combine pork and ground beef in a large, deep skillet over medium-high heat; cook and stir until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes. Add onion and cook until translucent, about 5 minutes. Stir in crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, garlic, basil, salt, oregano, and sugar. Reduce heat to medium-low and simmer, stirring occasionally, for 30 minutes.
  2. Cook the noodles: While the sauce is simmering, bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in the boiling water, stirring occasionally, until tender yet firm to the bite, 8 to 10 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  3. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, remaining 1 tablespoon fresh parsley, salt, and pepper in a large bowl until combined.
  4. Assemble the lasagna: Spread a spoon or two of sauce over the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish just to to coat it. Place two layers of noodles over the sauce to cover. Layer with 1/2 of the cheese mixture, 1/2 of the remaining sauce, and 1/2 of the mozzarella cheese. Repeat layers once more using the remaining noodles, cheese mixture, sauce, and mozzarella. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake the lasagna: Bake in the preheated oven for 30 to 40 minutes. Remove the foil and bake until cheese is golden brown, 5 to 10 more minutes.
  6. Serve: Remove from the oven and let stand for 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
