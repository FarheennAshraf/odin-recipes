Mayo Chicken

Description

This mayo chicken is a simple, tasty baked chicken dinner made with cut-up chicken coated with garlic-herb mayonnaise good for a low-carb diet. Use up the rest of your mayonnaise and make this Mayo Chicken. Not only can you experience the umami taste of mayo, but it's also balanced with black pepper, rosemary, garlic, and salt.

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into 6 pieces
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh rosemary
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • ½ cup grated Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Place chicken pieces into a 9x13-inch baking dish. Stir together mayonnaise, garlic, rosemary, salt, and black pepper in a bowl. Spread mayonnaise mixture over chicken; top with Parmesan cheese.
  3. Bake in the preheated oven until no longer pink in the center and the juices run clear, about 1 hour and 10 minutes. An instant-read thermometer inserted into the center should read at least 165 degrees F (74 degrees C).
  4. Remove from the oven and let it stand for a few minutes before serving
